Why Choose us?

If your go-to spot is still open, they may be willing to sell you some fresh food from their supply. This probably won't work for chains, but worth a shot for pizzerias (fresh dough for homemade pies), steakhouses (deals on steak cuts), sushi bars (different varieties of fish to mix up your menus), and others. As a response to the coronavirus, many small farms have increased their social media presence and started delivery services. CSA programs, which stand for 'Community Supported Agriculture,' offer organic produce sold directly from small farms. With CSAs, you buy a 'share' of the farm’s harvest in advance, which is then delivered to you once it’s harvested.
